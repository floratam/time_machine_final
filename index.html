SOPHIE was a Scottish music producer, songwriter and DJ.

This website serves as a snapshot into her discography including early projects, works under her main moniker, as well as notable collaborations (all in chronological order: from top to bottom, left to right.)

SFIRE (2013)
Sfire

As one of her earlier projects, Sfire (2013) was produced by both SOPHIE and Detroit based DJ Jeffrey Sfire under the moniker Sfire. This was the first collaboration EP between the two.

1. Sfire2 2. Sfire3 3. Sfire 3E

Nothing More To Say / Eeehhh (2013)

SOPHIE’s debut single was released in February 2013. Along with the release included the B-side “Eeehhh” and featured two mixes of the titular track, featuring vocals from Jaide Green.

A. Nothing More to Say (Dub/Vox) B. Eeehhh

BIPP (2013)

This follow-up single to Nothing More to Say / Eeehhh and featured vocals from her former bandmate, Marcella Dvsi. This single in particular became a critical success, with Pitchfork later ranking the track 56th on its list of the best tracks from 2010 - 2014. BIPP was also to become one of the songs in the Product compilation released in 2015.

Hey QT (2014)
QT

A collaboration with A.G. Cook, Harriet Pittard and Hayley Dunham, featuring a fictional energy drink called “QT Energy Elixir.” It is the only song created under this project.

Vroom Vroom (2015)
Charli XCX

As her first official collaboration with singer Charli XCX, this project features SOPHIE's signature production on the 4 track EP. It received mixed reviews due to its experimental sound, but has been retrospectively credited as a work that heavily influenced what was to become the hyperpop movement.

1. Vroom Vroom 2. Paradise (ft. Hannah Dianmond) 3. Trophy 4. Secret (Shh)

Product (2015)

A compilation album of 8 songs, four of which were released earlier on as singles from 2013-2015. The album features SOPHIE's collaborators from PC Music, GFOTY and Raffy on vocals.

1. BIPP 2. ELLE 3. LEMONADE 4. HARD 5. MSMSMSM 6. VYZEE 7. L.O.V.E. 8. JUST LIKE WE NEVER SAID GOODBYE

Yeah Right (2017)
Vince Staples

Released in 2017, this track was collaboration between rapper Vince Staples and music producer Flume, who produced the track alongside SOPHIE.

OIL OF EVERY PEARL'S UN-INSIDES(2017)

Nominated for Best Dance/Electronic Album at the 61st Annual Grammy Awards, OIL OF EVERY PEARL'S UN-INSIDES was the first and only studio album by SOPHIE. It was met with wide acclaim, with many publications citing the album as a top release of the year.

1. It's Okay To Cry 2. Ponyboy 3. Faceshopping 4. Is It Cold In The Water? 5. Infatuation 6. Not Okay 7. Pretending 8. Immaterial 9. Whole New World/Pretend World

Slime (2020)
Shygirl

Produced alongside Kai Whiston and Sega Bodega, Slime was the second single from Shygirl's 2020 EP Alias.
